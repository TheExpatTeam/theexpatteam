Welcome

Moving to Germany is exciting, but language barriers, unfamiliar bureaucracy and a new financial system can be overwhelming. The Expat Team is here to make your transition smoother, whether you’re a professional, student or family. We’ll help you open bank accounts, choose the right health, liability, life and home‑contents insurance, build credit, grow your savings and plan for your future.

As part of the Horbach network, our advisors understand the unique needs of expats and international students. We start by listening to your ideal life vision, then use simple cashflow models to map out your financial future. From your first week in Germany through every life stage, we’re by your side.

Our Services

Essential Insurance

Tailored advice on health, liability, life and home‑contents insurance that meets German requirements and fits your lifestyle.

Banking & Everyday Finance

Guidance on opening bank accounts, setting up digital payments and building credit – we help you navigate German bureaucracy so you can get on with life.

Family & Student Finance

Support with child benefits, student insurance and scholarships, budgeting for studies and family life, and planning ahead for your loved ones.

Real Estate & Loans

Explore property purchases, mortgages and financing options. We advise you on buying a home or obtaining a loan as an expat in Germany.

Wealth & Retirement

Build long‑term security with personalised saving, investing and old‑age provision strategies designed for expats.

Our Process

Our three‑step process starts with your goals. Contact us for a free appointment, where we discuss your situation and develop a personalised plan. We believe in transparency – no short‑term speculation or hidden fees – and ongoing support as your life evolves.
