About Us

Relocating to a new country is an exciting adventure—yet understanding Germany’s financial, insurance and bureaucratic systems can feel daunting. The Expat Team is here to guide you every step of the way. We help expats and international students open bank accounts, choose the right health, liability and life insurance, build credit, plan for retirement and invest wisely. Our mission is to simplify German finance so you can focus on building a life you love.

Our Services

Essential Insurance

Get tailored advice on health, liability and life insurance that meets German requirements and fits your lifestyle.

Banking & Credit

We guide you through opening bank accounts, setting up digital payment methods and building credit for future goals.

Wealth & Retirement

Plan for long‑term security with personalised strategies for saving, investing and old‑age provision in Germany.

Family & Lifestyle

Advice on home contents, family finances and loans—so you can protect what matters most while enjoying life here.
