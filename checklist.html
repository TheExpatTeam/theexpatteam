What to expect

Relocating to Germany comes with a host of practical tasks. Our checklist distils the essential steps you need to take when starting your life here. From sorting out paperwork and insurance to finding a home and building your network, we’ve got you covered.

Your Expat Checklist

  • Sign your employment contract and confirm start date
  • Apply for recognition of your qualifications if needed
  • Secure accommodation and review your rental agreement
  • Register your residence and pay the broadcasting fee (GEZ)
  • Take out appropriate health insurance coverage
  • Register with the tax office and obtain your tax ID and tax class
  • Open a German bank account
  • Obtain a social security number (Sozialversicherungsnummer)
  • Check your work permit and visa requirements
  • Transfer or exchange your driving licence
  • Enroll in a German language course
  • Arrange childcare or school places if applicable
  • Get private liability and household contents insurance
  • Build your network of professionals and local contacts
