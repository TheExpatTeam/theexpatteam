About Us

Moving to Germany is exciting, but navigating the financial system, bureaucracy and cultural differences can be challenging. At The Expat Team, we help you feel at home by simplifying important tasks like opening a bank account, choosing the right health, liability and life insurance and building long-term wealth.

We offer one-on-one consultations and workshops in English and German. Our experienced advisors have helped clients from over 15 countries understand German regulations and select the right coverage and investment strategies for every stage of life.

Our Services

  • Basic Insurance: health, liability and life insurance tailored for expats and students.
  • Finance for the family: protecting your loved ones and planning for children and education.
  • Real estate & loans: support with home financing and mortgages in Germany.
  • Old-age provision: pension planning and strategies for long-term security.
  • Wealth building: strategies to grow your savings through investing and savings plans.

Not sure where to start? Our Expat Guide includes step-by-step checklists for your first days in Germany and is free when you schedule a consultation.
